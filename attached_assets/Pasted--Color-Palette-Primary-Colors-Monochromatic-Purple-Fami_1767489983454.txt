- **#7763B7** - Core Brand Purple (logo, headlines, CTAs)
- **#9D8BBF** - Secondary Purple (section headers, hover states)
- **#CDBFF7** - Light Purple (backgrounds, accents)

### Secondary Colors (Accent & Contrast)

- **#C6A54E** - Gold (authority, premium, trust markers, CTA hover)
- **#EAD4A2** - Light Gold (subtle highlights, charts, secondary buttons)

### Neutral Colors

- **#342A4F** - Deep Purple (backgrounds, emphasis, data highlight)
- **#F0E6FA** - Light Purple Grey (backgrounds, dividers)
- **#E3DFE8** - Grey Lavender (forms, dividers)
- **#F0EDF4** - Off White (subtle backgrounds)
- **#FFFFFF** - White (clean space, high contrast for text)

**Usage Notes:**

- Use primarily monochromatic purple palette with varying saturation for depth
- Gold accent emphasizes key elements and guides attention
- Neutrals ensure accessibility and legibility

## Typography

### Primary Typeface: Work Sans

- **Use:** Headlines, key brand messaging, callouts
- **Weights:** ExtraBold (H1), SemiBold (H2), Regular (body emphasis)
- **Why:** Modern, geometric sans-serif that feels clean, trustworthy, and forward-thinking
- **Link:** [Adobe Fonts - Work Sans](https://fonts.adobe.com/fonts/work-sans)

### Secondary Typeface: Source Sans Pro

- **Use:** Body copy, captions, legal copy, UI text
- **Weights:** Regular (paragraph), Semibold (subhead emphasis)
- **Why:** Humanist sans-serif, highly readable for dense content and long-form copy
- **Link:** [Adobe Fonts - Source Sans](https://fonts.adobe.com/fonts/source-sans)

### Accent Typeface: Space Mono

- **Use:** Data presentation, code snippets, technical callouts
- **Weights:** Regular (inline references), Bold (highlighted data)
- **Why:** Monospaced but contemporary â€” creates precision and technical authority
- **Link:** [Adobe Fonts - Space Mono](https://fonts.adobe.com/fonts/space-mono)

### Font Hierarchy Example

- **H1 (Hero/Headline):** Work Sans ExtraBold 48pt
- **H2 (Subheading):** Work Sans SemiBold 28pt
- **Body Copy:** Source Sans Pro Regular 16pt
- **Captions/Legal:** Source Sans Pro Regular 12pt
- **Data/Technical:** Space Mono Bold 14pt
